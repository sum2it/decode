Ambition

By Sumit Srivastava on 22 December 2019

Decode was born as a place to hang out for lads who are ambitious, curious, or risk takers.

While it did become one of the largest inclusive communities in Uttar Pradesh, a state in India, it does not cover even a fraction of the most ambitious people in the world.

I will go on to describe where I got my ambition from and how you can get into the brains of the most ambitious people on earth, mostly by reading what they write and hanging out where the hang out.

  1. Why be ambitious? Your ambition essentially defines what you will do good at, what you'll master, what will interest you, and what will inspire you.

    Paul Graham, a major influence on my life and the founder of Ycombinator, writes great essays to read from. Here are two essays that I like:

    • Frighteningly Ambitious Goals

    • Cities And Ambition

    While startup ideas can be substituted with ambitious goals and cities can be substitued with internet communities, the gist of essay stays the same.

    A lot of ambitious people hang out at a lot of places on the internet. I am about to tell you where you can go to hang out with the ambitious people for the ambition you have, or for the things you want to master.

  2. What should you do with your life? I suggest you to read the essay Directions and advice from Alexey Guzey. Here is the essay broken down in parts:

    • What to work on?
    • How to actually work on the problem you like?
    • Cold emails and twitter
    • Where to find funding to work on any of these problems?
    • How to achieve anything?
